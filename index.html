What is PCP

Phencyclidine (PCP), also known as angel dust and Sernyl among others, is a dissociative drug. Wikipedia
In plain english, PCP is a drug that causes strong hallucinogenic effects.

Why is PCP so dangerous?

As PCP is a hallucinogenic, it can make the user do very strange and dangerous things.

These things include...

  • Changes in body image
  • Loss of ego boundaries
  • Paranoia
  • Depersonalization
  • Acting like a Zombie
  • Not responding
  • Seizures

What are the Symptoms?

Symptoms are summarized by the mnemonic device RED DANES:

  • Rage
  • Erythema (redness of skin)
  • Dilated pupils
  • Delusions
  • Amnesia
  • Nystagmus (oscillation of the eyeball when moving laterally)
  • Excitation
  • Skin dryness

Is PCP Addictive?

Yes, PCP is very addictive.
